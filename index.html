Welcome to My Portfolio!

Hello, visitor! My name is Charlie Sambalod Jr. I am a second-year IT student in University of Science and Technology of Philippines. I am at the age of 20 years old and I live Puerto, Cagayan de Oro. I have been exposed to computers at a very young age and I even learned to do basic video editing skills at that age. So it was a no-brainer that I made the decision of pursuing BS-IT in the college I'm attending to.
For the past several years, I've been doing Photoshop/Pixlr edits of anything (from school works to birthday cards) here and then. In this portfolio, I will showcase several images I make these edits either for fun or for turning in output to school. You might see chaotic images that are only understandable to few people in the images that I did for fun. For the ones I did to turn in output to school, you might expect a bit more professional with it.

"Istitik Website Project" "Daily Picture Diary"
The Istitk website project were made in collaboration with a few of my classmates in 1st Year and also with Elijah Pajaron. We made this website as a final requirement for our Art Appreciation course using Google Sites. All of the work including T-shirt designs, content creation and website engineering and design were divided among eight of us. The website engineering and design went to me and Elijah.
The Daily Picture Diary was a week-long task that was handed to us during Grade 11 for our Empowerment Technologies subject. We were tasked take any pictures during any time of the day and make any sort of changes/edits to the picture and repeat the task for seven days. As you can see in the album, there are two sets of pictures: the unedited and edited versions. My best edit is the one that is displayed on the website right now.
"Hindi Niya Kilala si Rizal" "Maguindanao Weaving Art (GD)"
